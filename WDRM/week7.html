Week 7

Problems

  1. Let \(a,b,c \in \mathrm{R^+}\) such that \(a +b +c = 1\). Prove that: \[ \frac{a}{a^2+b^3+c^3}+\frac{b}{a^3+b^2+c^3}+\frac{c}{a^3+b^3+c^2}\leq \frac{1}{5abc} \]
  2. Suppose \(x \in \mathrm{R}/\{0\}\) such that both \(x^5\) and \(20x + \frac{19}{x}\) are rational numbers. Prove that \(x\) is a rational number.
